About

Task Burndown is an agile approach to task management. It was written design to use the agile weight assignment and burndown charts to incentivize users to get things done in every area of their life.

Simple to use and built in AngularJS's new Material Design patterns, it will work on your mobile devices and in any browser, allowing you to keep your tasks on hand whereever you are.

Features
Task Backlog
Variable Sprints
Organization by category
Burndown Charts
Statistics on completion
